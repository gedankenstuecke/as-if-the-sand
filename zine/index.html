A5, 28 pages with 24 black-and-white images. Get a copy!

Our first zine includes an initial collection of black & white images that we made as part of this project in September 2023. The images cover a small slice of the towns, villages & hamlets in the provinces of Santa Fe & Buenos Aires. Beyond the images the zine contains a brief introductory text about the project in English & Spanish.

Print copies of the zine are available through our Ko-Fi page, or by getting in touch via email.

PDF copies can be found online here, and all images are also available in this set on Flickr. The images as well as the zine itself are openly licensed under a Creative Commons BY-SA license.
